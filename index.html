Farmer

The farmer can monitor the price at which the distributor is selling their product. farmer get adequate information of the price at which their product is being sold to the customers.

Distributor

The distributor would be able to locate the nearest and feasible farmer from whom the distributor can buy products. and can store the products which they have brought from the farmers.

Consumer

The Consumers can rate the distributors according to service and quality they are providing. Each customer can select the distributor based upon their ratings.

Features

Following points includes both functional and non functional Features of our app.

IoT Device

IoT based storage device to store the product. The weight sensors in the device will notify when the specific product is out of stock.

Database Connection

The application will be connected in the backend to a mysql database. The relevant tables are made with proper constraints placed on them.

Bidding Option

While purchasing certain vergetables from the farmers, consumers will be provided with the bargaining option.

Machine Learning

ML is used for predicting the price at which the farmer can sell their product. it helps the distributor to buy the product from the farmer.

Map UI

The Farmers,distributor and the cosumers are be provided with the map UI. The distributor would be able to locate the nearest and feasible farmer from whom the distributor can buy products.

More Features

cart option for both customers and Farmers,wide variety of payment option,transparent flow of distribution cycle.
